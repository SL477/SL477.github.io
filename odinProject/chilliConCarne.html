Description

This is quite a long & complicated recipe with some multi-tasking.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 tsp hot chilli powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 500g minced beef
  • 1 stock cube
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 0.5 tsp marjoram
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 400g red kidney beans

Steps

  1. Prepare vegetables
  2. Start cooking the main pan, add oil then when it's warmed up add the onion & cook for 5 minutes
  3. Start the cooking the meat in a second pan. Try to break it up and cook until it is browned.
  4. Add the garlic, red pepper, chilli powder, paprika & cumin. Stir and cook for 5 minutes, occasionally stirring.
  5. Add the browned meat to the mixture, you may need to turn the heat up. Cook for 5 more minutes.
  6. Crumble the stock cube into 300ml hot water. Add this to the mixture, along with the chopped tomatoes, marjoram, sugar, salt, pepper & tomato purée. Simmer it gently, bring it to the boil, stir and put a lid on it. Turn down the heat until it is gently bubbling & leave for 20 minutes.
  7. Drain & rinse the red kidney beans, then stir into the chilli pot. Bring to the boil again and bubble for 10 minutes.
  8. Taste & add more seasoning. Then leave for another 10 minutes.
