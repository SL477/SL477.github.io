It was supposed to be an easy job.
Sly O'Hara kept his hands pressed to his face.
From a distance it looked like he was crying, when you got closer it was blood running out from the ruin of his face.
Gunther dragged him down the filthy alley, litter billowing in their wake, Pretty Micky tailed them and sent a couple of shots back at their pursuers.
The warehouse was meant to have been empty.
Jessie had died within thirty seconds of getting into the warehouse.
And then the second shotgun blast had caught Sly in the face.
"Chris get to the end of the alley now!" Gunther yelled. Sly ran blindly, having to trust that Gunther would guide him well, his face ached dully the shock numbing the pain.
He flinched at the sound of a shotgun blast, it was far too far away to do any damage. Mickey's shot taught the rest of the pursuers to back off a bit.
He heard the pur of the van's electric motors as Chris stopped there.
Sly's leg got bruised as Gunther shoved him into the back of it.
Gunther laid down covering fire for Pretty Micky, who jumped in.
Gunther slammed the doors and Chris drove off.

The electric van tore off, there were a few pings as their pursuers fired on them.
Sly collapsed onto the floor.
He was barely conscious when they dragged him into Dr Ahab's office.
Sly's blindness meant that he couldn't see what the Doc was doing.
Which given Ahab's love for drink meant that his hands shook a lot, fortunately his surgical robot was doing a lot of the work.
The ruins of his eyes came out.
Two robotic eyes went in, the surgical robot bonding them to Sly's optic nerves.
Sly suddenly saw the filthy ceiling when they were fully connected.
He attempted to scream in agony, the doc had put a nerve block on him that had fully paralysed him, he had not been given anything for the pain.
Ahab brought a roll of skin off of the printer, there would be no fear of rejection as the DNA they were grown from was Sly's.
The surgical robot enthusiastically dropped over Sly's face and its many hands cut and stitched.
"All better Mr O'Hara.
I suggest not falling down the stairs again."
He said sarcastically, not believing Gunther for a single second.
He showed Sly his work.
His face was now baby smooth, the scar on his cheek gone, it contrasted vividly with the rest of his head.
Below the nose it went back to his prematurely aged skin and again up at his hairline you could see his original flesh.
A pair of silver eyes looked out at the world, his new eyelids blinked over them, a very weird feeling.
His eyelashes were amongst what the surgical robot was cleaning away, they would grow again.
Gunther had handed over most of the gang's money, but a new set of biological eyes would have been far too much (painkillers and replacement blood had also been deemed as outside the budget, but that was more cruelty on Ahab's part).

In the alley outside Doc Ahab's excuse for a surgery Sly looked up.
His new eyes let him see more than his biological eyes ever had.
He noticed a slight infrared glow in the sky, this was from the solar sail which currently blotted out the sun.
The distant glow of lights could be seen on the other side of the habitat ring.
