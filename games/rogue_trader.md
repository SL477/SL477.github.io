How to sum up the life of the noble Rogue Trader Darius Von Valensius, bearer of the holy warrant of trade, saviour of Dagonus. Once I was nought but a humble crime lord. Then my great aunt Theodora recognised my natural heroism and recruited me to be her heir[^1]. She may have been a despicable heretic, but she ensured that the sacred warrant of trade fell into my noble hands[^2].
On Rykad Minoris I met Interegor Heinrix Van Calex, who I separated from Lord Inquisitor Defo Heretic[^3].
Abelard Werserian, my faithful companion was by my side the whole time. I saved his family from accusations of heresy and allowed him to keep his silly rituals on my ship.
Jae Heydari joined my noble crusade across the Koronus Expanse at Footfall Station. Together we stole everything that wasn't nailed down across the expanse, and plenty of things that were.

I played as an iconoclast, helping the Koronus Expanse (those bits that survived me). On the grounds that I stole/reclaimed everything I didn't discover the trade section until the end. My many colonies benefited from my patronage.

As a game I don't think many have a character with as much personal power as Rogue Trader. There isn't really a money system, your character controls entire star systems and has a ship with thousands of people on it, more a system of bartering.
I would have liked to be able to explore the ship a bit more, after the prologue you only have access to the bridge.
The combat system left much to desired, you either steam rolled over everyone or got steam rolled in turn. The equipment also left much to be desired, as I had enough equipment to fight a small war but it was hard to work out whether you had upgrades for characters' weaponry or armour.

[^1]: and some other bloke. Who disinherited himself by getting himself killed and certainly had no assistance from me.

[^2]: the Emperor's divine justice found her.

[^3]: whose name is stricken from the records, as I never bothered to learn it.
