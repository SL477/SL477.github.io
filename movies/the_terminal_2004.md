The tragic/comic trail of Victor, trapped stateless in a New York airport when his country ceases to exist. He has to learn English and earn money in the airport. Loosely based on a true story.

Having spent a fair amount of time in airports I can certainly sympathise with the indefinite and arbitrary waits that airports impose at their own whim. I did enjoy the story, hopefully no one else has to go through actually living at an airport.
