I like just about anything involving spaceships and apart from the Xenomorph running around it the Notromous is perfect. Probably, if they turned the lighting up so you could see what was going on [^1].
Until recently the idea of a computer like Mother taking up a whole room seemed preposterous. But now with LLMs and their backers wanting to convert the solar system into a Dyson Sphere data centre because efficiency is overrated, it's not looking that preposterous anymore.
Retro futurism is brilliant, Alien doesn't do it as well as Thunderbirds but few things do. And you can see the inspiration that Red Dwarf took for their show[^2]. I'm sure in years to come people will look back at our shows/movies set in the future and say 'how quaint, they had no idea about [unknown future technology]'

[^1]: a criticism I have for all Alien, Predator and Alien Vs Predator and whatever other things they've invaded now. They are something of an audiobook.

[^2]: Polymorph anyone?
