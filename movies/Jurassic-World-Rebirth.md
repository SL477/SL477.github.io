I feel that Jurassic Park/World films get progressively worse.
But this is better than Dominion (not by much).
There are a lot more dinosaurs, but more isn't better.
There was a token attempt to show continuity by showing some dinosaurs in the wild, but apparently the dinosaurs moved elsewhere of their own volition.

The genetic hybrid dinosaurs are back.
Looking like they borrowed the rancour from star wars and then crossed a pterodactyl with a velociraptor.

Not sure who would think it was a good idea to sail your kids straight through dinosaur country, but that's one of the plot points.
And why do people need samples from the dinosaurs for medicine?
As InGen grew all of the dinosaurs, so somewhere someone has all of the dinosaur DNA on file and computers normally don't try to eat you when you use them.
