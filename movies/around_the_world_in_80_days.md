A weird and wacky steampunk comedy loosely based on the Jules Verne book.
Phious Fogg tries to travel around the world in 80 days.

One of the most brilliant movies I've ever seen!
It has Kung Ku fighting, trains, wacky inventions and piles of laughs.
A great wiz through random countries.
