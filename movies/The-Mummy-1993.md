A brilliant film a weird mix of humour and horror, Brendan Fraser is excellent at O'Connell the swashbuckling hero.

We see random bits of Egypt from the old museum in Cairo, the Pyramids and Harmanatra (which is made up).

The film doesn't take itself too seriously, which certainly works with the humour.
