Not quite as good as the first film, but InGen comes in with a load more equipment as they come to grab as much as they can from Isla Nublar.

Richard Hammond's transformation into environmentalist is remarked on a bit fantastical.
He sends Ian Malcolm and co to do a wildlife documentary on the dinosaurs on Site B (those on Site A have perished).

InGen's second smash and grab team is huge, unfortunately that is just more food for dinosaurs.

The RV that Ian Malcolm's team take with them looks like it could be one of the coolest vehicles in film, my only criticism is that we don't see that much of it.

Tyrannosaurus Rex is the stand out star of the film, we have two adults and an infant.
The velociraptors also make a good showing.
